The William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition

Announcement of winners of the fifty-fifth competition held on December 3, 1994

WINNING TEAMS

  1. HARVARD UNIVERSITY: Kiran S. Kedlaya, Lenhard L. Ng, Dylan P. Thurston
  2. CORNELL UNIVERSITY: Jeremy L. Bem, Robert D. Kleinberg, Mark Krosky
  3. MASSACHUSETTS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY: Henry L. Cohn, Adam W. Meyerson, Thomas A. Weston
  4. PRINCETON UNIVERSITY: William R. Mann, Joel E. Rosenberg, Michail Sunitsky
  5. UNIVERSITY OF WATERLOO: Ian A. Goldberg, Peter L. Milley, Kevin Purbhoo
The first place team receives an award of $7,500 and each member of the team receives $500. The awards for second place are $5,000 and $400; for third place $3,000 and $300; for fourth place $2,000 and $200; and for fifth place $1000 and $100.

In each of the following categories, the listing is in alphabetical order.
