1994 Putnam Competition Score Breakdowns

The following table correlates raw scores on the competition with ranks. (In case of a tie, the average rank is given. That's why some ranks are not integers.) Your school's Putnam advisor should have your raw score (unless you're a Putnam Fellow, in which case your score is not released). Remember that teams are compared by the total of their ranks (lower is better), not of their raw scores. (Why are there nonintegral scores between 28 and 29? Your guess is as good as mine, but they look like they were used to break the tie near 200th place.)
